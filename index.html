It's time for a different course of action in Oklahoma. One that puts people before party, community before corporate interests, and hope before division. Join me in this journey to create a brighter, more equitable, and more prosperous future for all Oklahomans.

Cost of Housing

Affordable Housing for All Oklahomans

I am committed to making housing accessible and affordable for every Oklahoman. The dream of home ownership is out of reach for many and high housing costs are effecting us all and leading to high levels of homelessness and eviction. The solution is to increase housing supply, lower the concentration of housing ownership by corporate and private interests, and provide tax breaks to homeowners as well as subsidies and down payment assistance to first-time home buyers.

I will introduce a comprehensive subsidy program to help first-time homebuyers and low-income families achieve the dream of homeownership. This includes down payment assistance, low-interest loans, and tax credits for home purchases.

My weatherization grant program will help homeowners improve insulation and energy efficiency. This will help reduce utility costs and improve comfort. This includes funding window upgrades, and HVAC improvements, up-to-date insulation techniques, grass roofs, and solar with a focus on those receiving disability benefits, low-income, and elderly residents.

I'll work to implement fair and balanced measures to protect tenants from excessive rent increases while ensuring property owners can maintain their properties. This includes annual rent increase caps tied to inflation and mediation services for landlord-tenant disputes.

To discourage the concentration of property ownership and promote a more diverse housing market, I'll introduce an additional property tax on individuals or companies owning more than two residential units. This will help create opportunities for more people to become homeowners.

We'll expand housing assistance programs for individuals receiving Supplemental Security Income (SSI) or enrolled in Soonercare. This includes rental assistance, specialized housing options, and support services to ensure our most vulnerable residents have safe and stable housing.

We'll establish a state-level fund to support municipalities in building and maintaining affordable housing. This fund will provide grants and low-interest loans to local governments for the development of mixed-income housing projects, renovation of existing properties, and infrastructure improvements in underserved areas.

Education

Empowering Our Future Through Education

As your representative, I'm committed to revolutionizing our education system. Every child in Oklahoma deserves a quality education that prepares them for the future. Here's how I plan to make that happen:

I'll work tirelessly to overhaul the OSDE, focusing on:

  • • Streamlining bureaucratic processes to reduce waste and increase efficiency
  • • Implementing data-driven decision-making to improve educational outcomes
  • • Enhancing support for teachers and school districts
  • • Developing a transparent system for resource allocation

I believe in the power of democracy, especially when it comes to our children's education. Here's my plan:

  • • Introduce legislation to make Board of Education positions elected rather than appointed
  • • Ensure diverse representation from all educational backgrounds
  • • Implement term limits to encourage fresh perspectives
  • • Create a transparent campaign process for board elections

Our teachers are the backbone of our education system. I propose:

  • • Increasing teacher salaries to be competitive with neighboring states
  • • Providing more resources for classroom supplies and technology
  • • Offering ongoing professional development opportunities
  • • Implementing a mentorship program for new teachers

Together, we can build an education system that truly serves our children and our communities. I'm committed to working with parents, teachers, and education experts to make these reforms a reality.

Cost of Living/Food

Making Life Affordable for All Oklahomans

I'm committed to addressing the rising cost of living and ensuring that every Oklahoman can afford basic necessities, including food and healthcare. Here's how I plan to make that happen:

I support the petition and will vote yes on the ballot. If that should fail I will work to enact the same policies in the Oklahoma State Senate.

Healthcare costs are spiraling out of control, and it's time we did something about it. I'm proposing legislation to cap annual increases in health insurance premiums at 5% or the rate of inflation, whichever is lower. This will prevent insurers from gouging consumers and ensure that healthcare remains accessible to all Oklahomans. Additionally, I'll push for increased transparency in healthcare pricing to help consumers make informed decisions, including publishing price plans.Comprehensive coverage should include Dental and Vision With limits on the procedures insurance companies can deny coverage for.

No one should go bankrupt due to a medical emergency or natural disaster. I'm proposing the creation of a state-level Catastrophic Insurance Fund to provide a safety net for Oklahomans facing extreme financial hardships due to unforeseen circumstances. This fund will help cover costs that exceed typical insurance limits, ensuring that our neighbors don't lose everything due to a single catastrophic event. Insurance is supposed to spread the cost for the benefit of us all but far too often GoFundMe accounts are used to cover what insurance wont. This needs to end.

No Oklahoman should go to bed hungry. I'm committed to expanding food security programs, including increasing funding for SNAP benefits and school meal programs. I'll also work to create incentives for grocery stores to open in food deserts and support local farmers' markets to increase access to fresh, affordable produce in all communities. Neiborhood Assoctions would receive funding for community gardens and funding for a paid position Of “Neighboorhood Farmer” to ensure food scarcity is A thing of the past. We should incentivize bringing rural farming communities and urban food deserts together.

Election and Voting Reform

Revitalizing Oklahoma's Democratic Process

As your representative, I'm committed to modernizing and strengthening our democratic process in Oklahoma. Our state's future depends on a fair, accessible, and robust voting system that ensures every voice is heard and every vote counts.

Why Voting Reform Matters in Oklahoma

Oklahoma has consistently ranked among the states with the lowest voter turnout. This isn't just a statistic – it's a challenge we need to address. By reforming our voting system, we can:

  • Increase civic engagement and voter participation
  • Ensure our elected officials truly represent the will of the people
  • Build trust in our democratic institutions
  • Empower citizens to have a stronger voice in shaping our state's future

Explore Alternative Voting Methods

I'm advocating for a more representative voting system that gives voters more choice. Here's how we'll approach this:

  • • Propose legislation to create pilot programs testing alternative voting methods like Ranked Choice Voting
  • • Work with the State Election Board to study the feasibility and impact of different voting systems
  • • Educate voters on how these systems work and their potential benefits
  • • Ensure our voting infrastructure is prepared for potential changes

Improve the Initiative Petition Process

Oklahomans deserve a more accessible way to directly influence our laws. I plan to:

  • • Propose legislation to simplify the initiative petition process
  • • Advocate for extending the time allowed for signature gathering
  • • Push for the creation of an online system for signature verification
  • • Work to ensure the process is fair and transparent

Promote Voter Participation

To boost voter turnout and emphasize the importance of civic participation, I propose:

  • • Advocating for policies that make voting more accessible, such as expanded early voting options
  • • Working with employers to ensure workers have time to vote
  • • Supporting voter education initiatives
  • • Exploring the feasibility of automatic voter registration

These reforms are crucial for ensuring that our government truly represents the will of all Oklahomans. By modernizing our voting system, we can build a more engaged, responsive, and democratic Oklahoma.

Federal Funding

Maximizing Resources for Oklahoma

I'm committed to ensuring Oklahoma makes the most of available federal resources to support crucial programs and initiatives. Here's how I plan to make that happen:

I'll work tirelessly to ensure Oklahoma takes advantage of all available federal funding opportunities. This includes:

  • Collaborating with our congressional delegation to identify federal resources
  • Working with state agencies to improve grant writing and management capabilities
  • Advocating for the acceptance of beneficial federal programs
  • Ensuring transparency in how federal funds are used and their impact on our state

It's time we prioritize the health of Oklahomans. I propose:

  • Advocating for the full implementation of Medicaid expansion
  • Working to leverage federal funds to improve our rural healthcare infrastructure
  • Pushing for increased investment in preventive care programs
  • Ensuring that mental health and substance abuse treatment receive adequate funding

Our state's infrastructure needs significant investment. I'll work to:

  • Advocate for increased federal infrastructure grants for roads, bridges, and public transportation
  • Push for increased funding for rural broadband expansion
  • Seek support for renewable energy projects and grid modernization
  • Ensure Oklahoma gets its fair share of disaster preparedness and relief funding

We'll advocate for a comprehensive program to improve safety in schools and rural communities across Oklahoma. This initiative will include:
  • Pushing for grants for schools to build or upgrade storm shelters
  • Advocating for funding for community storm shelters in rural areas
  • Encouraging partnerships with local contractors to ensure efficient construction
  • Promoting regular maintenance and inspection of existing shelters

To better protect Oklahomans from severe weather events, we'll advocate for increased funding for early warning systems. This approach will include:
  • Supporting upgrades and expansion of weather radars and sensors
  • Advocating for implementation of advanced storm prediction tools
  • Pushing for improvements to emergency alert systems
  • Supporting regular public education campaigns on severe weather preparedness

We'll push to ensure that our 911 emergency response system is state-of-the-art and fully staffed. Our plan includes advocating for:
  • Upgrades to Next Generation 911 (NG911) systems statewide
  • Increased funding for 911 operator salaries to attract and retain skilled professionals
  • Implementation of comprehensive training programs for all 911 staff
  • Expansion of mental health support services for 911 operators

By maximizing our federal funding opportunities and advocating for crucial programs, we can invest in critical areas for the benefit of all Oklahomans. I'm committed to being a strong voice for Oklahoma's interests at all levels of government.

Sustainability

Building a Greener, Sustainable Oklahoma

Our climate is changing, and we need to act now. I'm committed to promoting sustainable practices and renewable energy to ensure a cleaner, greener future for Oklahoma. This path is about creating jobs, boosting our economy, and securing our energy independence while preserving our environment for future generations.

Oklahoma has tremendous potential for wind and solar energy. Here's what I'm proposing:

  • • Advocate for extending and expanding tax incentives for wind and solar energy production
  • • Work towards grid-scale battery implementations in Oklahoma, like those in Texas
  • • Push for investment in grid modernization to support renewable energy integration
  • • Promote green job training programs to support the transition to clean energy

We need to encourage environmentally responsible practices. Here's my plan:

  • • Propose legislation to create tax incentives for businesses adopting sustainable practices
  • • Advocate for increased funding for environmental conservation projects
  • • Support programs that educate the public on sustainable living practices
  • • Explore state-level incentives for businesses to reduce carbon emissions

Water is life, and we need to protect it. Here's what I'm proposing:

  • • Push for the development of a comprehensive state water management plan
  • • Advocate for stricter regulations on industrial water use and pollution
  • • Support investment in water infrastructure improvements and leak detection
  • • Promote water-efficient agricultural practices through education and incentives

We need to explore ways to improve our transportation system for better connectivity and accessibility. Here's the plan:

  • • Advocate for feasibility studies on improved rail connections across the state
  • • Support exploration of public transportation expansion options in major urban areas
  • • Push for improvements to bus lines and pedestrian accessibility with a focus on disability access
  • • Promote comprehensive transportation planning that considers environmental impact

We have one shot at this. Our kids and grandkids are counting on us to get this right. I'm committed to working with experts, industry leaders, and everyday Oklahomans to build a sustainable future that works for all of us.
